Master Thesis

  • © Photo by Tomasz Zajda

    • Abstract

    Energy is essential for all life to exist and function properly. That is even more so true considering our modern society today, which is truly dependent on electricity, and as our global population increases by the minute, so will the energy demand of the world. A renewable energy resource that has the possibility to solve all the energy problems we face today is the sun. Humanity's way of harnessing the energy of the sun is through the use of solar cells, which converts sunlight into electricity. The materials that are being used today either are to inefficient or too expensive, so there are need for new materials in the development of better solar cells. Thus the focus of this thesis will be the exploratory work on new types of materials to be used for solar cells applications.
